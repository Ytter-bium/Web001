● About Us

Company Profile

Clear Path is a deep-tech startup founded in 2020 by engineers from leading robotics research labs. We specialize in intelligent scheduling algorithms and autonomous material recovery systems.
Our core innovation — dual-mode priority scheduling (autonomous dynamic & user-defined) — allows robots to either adapt to real-time environments or follow strict task orders. This flexibility makes our robots ideal for educational competitions, industrial sorting lines, and research labs.
